Pitbull History

The bull and terrier breeds were created in the 19th century for bull and bear baiting. Later dog fighting was where these dogs were used for hence their trait of aggression. In 1898, the AKC renamed the bull dogs to American Pit Bull Terrier.

Tips and Hints

    Pitbulls are not a good choice for people who can give them little or no attention
    Must be kept on a leash in public to prevent aggression toward other dogs
    Must be trained and socialized when young to overcome the breed's tendencies twoards stubborness and bossiness
